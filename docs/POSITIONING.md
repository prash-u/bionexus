# Positioning

BioNexus is a biological reasoning workspace, not an AI diagnosis app.

It supports education, exploratory simulation, hypothesis generation, biological relationship mapping and scientific communication.

It does not diagnose, predict disease, recommend treatment, produce patient-specific treatment plans, provide clinical decision support or claim clinical validation.

The product direction is closer to Figma for Biology, Notion for Biological Reasoning and Obsidian for Systems Biology: a coherent workspace that reduces context switching across literature, pathway, network, body-system and reporting tools.
